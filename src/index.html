ATTACK OF THE DESTRUCTOIDS!!!

SCORE: 
200
HIGH SCORE: 
300

OBJECTIVE:  Defend the nuclear reserves from the evil Destructoids' bombs. Use your bomb-resistant TerraDefence™ X-400 Bouncer-Shooter to either block them or shoot them down.


CONTROLS:  <z> rotate turret left
<x> fire
<c> rotate right
<left arrow> move left
<right arrow> move right
<p> pause


SCORING:  50 points for every enemy blocked.
20 points for every enemy shot down.
Every shot fired costs one point.
